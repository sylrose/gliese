Where the Gliese Art Collective's work has been displayed, from Virginia to the Phillippines.

- Publishers for Palestine's article "Zines, Books, and More: Free Reads for a Free Palestine"

For 2023's #ReadPalestine week, we were cited in an article promoting free zines by the organization Publishers for Palestine. View here!

- Student Union of Nova Scotia College of Art and Design zine press (SUNSCAD)'s 2025 June issue

Our zines were recently featured in the 2025 June issue of NSCAD's zine press. These zines focus on contemporary issues and struggles facing students, and are available at all three campuses as well as online. Check it out here.

- Owens Art Gallery at Mount Allison University (23-24)

Our Palestinian Art zine displayed in the 2023-2024 Gallery. View here.

- Displayed at Van Nuys Public Library's 'Read Palestine Week'

Our Palestine zine was displayed at Van Nuys Public Library in Los Angeles, California. The event inspired discussion about censorship. Read the article we are included in at The San Fernando Valley Sun.

- DC Collaborative's June 2025 Newsletter

Our organization was featured in DC Collaborative, a member service organization that supports cultural institutions, individual practitioners, and other related professionals in DC. Check it out here.

- Storygraph's Decolonize Your Shelf Challenge

Our zines were featured in this reading challenge on a website with 4 million users, a great Goodreads alternative! Check out the reading challenge here.

- Glasgow Zine Library

Our zines are cited in Glasgow, Scotland's Zine Library, founded in 2018, and featured in their Palestine Collection. Check it out here.

- Lasallians for Palestine's booth exhibit for ART4PALESTINE (De La Salle University, Manila)

Our Palestinian Art zine was in exhibit at a booth (by Anakbayan Vito Cruz) from June 18 until June 21 in support of Palestinian voices and narratives located at De La Salle University in Manila, Phillippines. View here.

- Pleasure Pie's article "Free Zines about Palestine"

Pleasure Pie cited our Palestinian Art zine as "A mini zine about Palestinian street art, music, photography, and more. Made by Gliese Art Collective." View here.

- Small Neighborhood Library, Zines to Share

A page sharing free one-page zines. View here.

- Huda Al Sousi Memorial Library (Naarm/Melbourne, AU)

Our Palestinian Art zine was printed and distributed for free at Huda Al Sousi Memorial Library.

- Distributed at PCRF fundraisers, DC protests, and Seattle protests for Palestine
